## Publications

### Adaptive Trust Consensus for Blockchain IoT

- **Year:** 2025 (ongoing extension)
- **Link:** [https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.22860](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.22860)

- **Year:** 2025
